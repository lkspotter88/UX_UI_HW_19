Hello, I am Linda Potter

As a UX researcher, I enjoy solving user problems through seeking to understand user backgrounds and motivations and developing solutions that more effectively connect users to the technology upon which they depend.

Linda's Story

From childhood, Linda was fascinated with technology. She studied computer science in both high school and college and enjoyed a 3-year career as a software developer at AT&T. She enjoyed the challenge of solving problems with code and designing tools to benefit the development team.

When a family member started an independent medical practice, Linda turned her problem-solving skills from code to people. Interacting with people from diverse backgrounds, she learned to present technical medical information in a way suited to the person with whom she was interacting. She also learned to ask probing, but not leading, questions to gain an understanding of patient problems and communicate this information to the medical providers. The best outcomes were achieved when the root of the problem could be identified and treated rather than just treating visible symptoms.

Now, Linda brings these skill sets to uncover user pain points and work with designers to create solutions that will provide users of technology with both a successful and stress-free experience.
